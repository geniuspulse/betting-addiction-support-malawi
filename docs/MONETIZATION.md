# BASM Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Freemium Subscriptions
- **Free tier** — always free, covers core recovery tools
- **Premium** — MK7,500/month, unlocks counsellor sessions, AI companion, training certificates
- Payment via: Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba, Bank Transfer

### 2. Counsellor Revenue Share
- Users pay counsellors directly through the platform
- Split: **80% counsellor / 20% platform**
- Session rates (set by counsellor, minimum MK8,000):
  - Video session: MK15,000 recommended
  - Chat session: MK8,000 recommended
  - Group session: MK3,000/person recommended
- Payouts: Every Friday via mobile money

### 3. Training Program Partnerships
- MFI/NGO partners pay to feature their products to graduates
- Sponsored training tracks (e.g., a bank sponsors the Financial Literacy track)

### 4. Institutional/NGO Contracts
- White-label or managed service for:
  - Malawi Ministry of Health
  - NGOs (World Vision, Catholic Relief Services, etc.)
  - Employers with staff wellness programs
- Pricing: negotiated per contract (MK5M–MK20M/year)

### 5. Grant Funding (Early Stage)
- UNDP Malawi digital health grants
- Gates Foundation financial inclusion
- Tony Elumelu Foundation
- Comic Relief / UK Aid addiction support grants

## Unit Economics (Example)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Premium subscription | MK7,500/month |
| Average sessions/user/month | 2 |
| Platform fee per session (MK15,000) | MK3,000 |
| Combined ARPU (premium + sessions) | ~MK13,500/month |
| Target: 1,000 premium users | MK13.5M/month revenue |

## Counsellor Earnings Example

A counsellor doing 5 sessions/week:
- 5 × MK15,000 = MK75,000/week gross
- Counsellor earns: MK60,000/week (80%)
- Platform earns: MK15,000/week (20%)
- Monthly counsellor income: ~MK240,000

This is competitive with clinical psychology salaries in Malawi.

## Payment Infrastructure
- **Mobile Money**: Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba (primary)
- **Bank Transfer**: National Bank of Malawi
- **Future**: Stripe for international donors/NGOs
